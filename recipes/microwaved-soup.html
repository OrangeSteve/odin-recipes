Microwaved Soup

Description

This treat will warm you up even on the coldest of days. A tangy and flavoursome tomato broth which will leave you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • Tinned Soup

Steps

  1. Open your tinned soup and pur into a microwavable container
  2. Heat in the microwave on full power for 2 minutes
  3. Stir
  4. Heat in the microwave for a further minute
  5. Stir and leave to stand for a minute
  6. Enjoy
