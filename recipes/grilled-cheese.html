Grilled Cheese

Description

An All-American classic. Two gooey slices of All-American Swiss cheese sandwiched between two fluffy yet crispy slices of All-American French bread. Transport your self to a simpler time with this staple treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Cheese
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up your skillet.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread butter side down in the skillet and top with the cheese.
  4. Place the other slice of bread butter side up on top of the cheese.
  5. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown then flip
  6. Continue cooking until the other slice is golden brown
