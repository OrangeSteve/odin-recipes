Beans on Toast

Description

A childhood favourite which always hits the spot.

Ingredients

  • Tinned Beans in tomato sauce
  • 3 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Open your tinned beans and put into a microwavable container
  2. Heat in the microwave on full power for 2 minutes
  3. While they are cooking place your bread in the toaster and toast until golden brown
  4. Stir the beans and heat in the microwave for a further minute
  5. Stir and leave to stand for a minute
  6. Place the toasted bread carefully on your plate before drowning the slices in beans and sauce
  7. Enjoy
